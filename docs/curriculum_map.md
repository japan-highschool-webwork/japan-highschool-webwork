# Curriculum Map for Japanese High-School Mathematics (Math I)

This document outlines the structure of the Japanese national curriculum  
for high-school mathematics (Math I), and the corresponding WeBWorK  
problem development plan.

---

## Unit 1: 数と式 (Expressions and Algebra)
- Numerical expressions  
- Polynomial operations  
- Factoring  
- Rational expressions  

---

## Unit 2: 二次関数 (Quadratic Functions)
- Graphs  
- Vertex form  
- Transformations  
- Roots and discriminant  

---

## Unit 3: 図形と計量 (Geometry & Measurement)
- Right triangles  
- Trigonometric ratios  
- Distance and angles  

---

## Unit 4: データの分析 (Data Analysis)
- Variance & standard deviation  
- Correlation  
- Regression line
